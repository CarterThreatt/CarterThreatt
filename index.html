About Me

Carter Threatt is a lifelong resident of Rock Hill, South Carolina, and a proud graduate of Rock Hill High School. With a passion for leadership and technology, Carter is currently pursuing a degree in Computer Information Systems at the University of South Carolina. He plans to attend law school after completing his undergraduate studies, aiming to build a career that combines his expertise in technology with legal knowledge. Carter serves as the Director of Technology for two local nonprofits, where he oversees digital initiatives and technology infrastructure. Additionally, he is the Director of Operations for a long-term home rentals company, managing day-to-day operations and ensuring the smooth running of the business. Outside of his professional and academic roles, Carter is actively involved in community service, continuing his work with local nonprofits and through Shandon Baptist Church in Columbia.
